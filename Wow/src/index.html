Welcome to WOW Hair Salon

WOW Hair Salon, the first salon of its kind in Warren (13 mile and Hoover), invites you to engage in an inspiring and rejuvenating experience. We offer a upbeat and trendy salon environment, with a lofty feel, where clients can expect cutting edge and personalized hair services from talented and creative artist.

Services

Providing the highest quality of hair care services has always been the trademark of Excel Hair. Since our opening our salon in 2014, we have established a reputation for providing innovative, contemporary and quality hair services. And continually improving our outstanding reputation is our top company priority.
